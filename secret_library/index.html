What's inside

Six collections of ancient writings

The Apocrypha

A curated collection of ancient biblical writings filled with wisdom, history, prophecy, courage, repentance, and divine deliverance. These texts open a window into sacred stories and traditions preserved outside many modern Bible editions.

The Book of Enoch

One of the most mysterious and influential ancient writings ever preserved, exploring Enoch's visions of heaven, fallen angels, the Watchers, the Nephilim, divine judgment, and the hidden mysteries of the spiritual realm.

The First Book of Adam and Eve

A powerful ancient narrative that continues the story of Adam and Eve after their expulsion from Eden. It reveals their sorrow, repentance, temptations, spiritual battles, and longing to return to the presence of God.

The Lost Books of the Bible

A fascinating collection of early Christian and extra-biblical writings, including forgotten gospels, apostolic letters, ancient traditions, and stories connected to the world of the first believers.

The Forgotten Books of Eden

A rare collection of ancient writings connected to Eden, Adam and Eve, Enoch, Solomon, the patriarchs, and early spiritual traditions. These texts reveal forgotten stories of creation, exile, wisdom, angels, and divine mystery.

Testaments

A collection of final teachings attributed to the ancient patriarchs, filled with moral warnings, prophetic visions, spiritual counsel, and reflections on virtue, temptation, repentance, and righteousness.
